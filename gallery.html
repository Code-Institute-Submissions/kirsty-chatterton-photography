About

Hi, I'm Kirsty and I’m a hobbyist photographer.
Originally born and bred in Northern Ireland, I currently live and work in Devon as a full-time musician and social media representative in The Band of Her Majesty’s Royal Marines. As well as photographing events within the band, I can always be found with a camera in my hand capturing a range of styles such as landscapes, pets and street photography.
